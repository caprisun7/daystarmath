# Quadratic Factorisation Worksheet

## Questions: Factorise the following expressions:

1. $$ x^2 + 13x + 30 $$
2. $$ x^2 + 13x - 30 $$
3. $$ x^2 - 13x + 30 $$

4. $$ 2x^2 + 13x + 15 $$
5. $$ 2x^2 - 13x + 15 $$
6. $$ 2x^2 - 13x - 15 $$

7. $$ 3x^2 + 13x + 10 $$
8. $$ 3x^2 + 13x - 10 $$
9. $$ 3x^2 - 13x - 10 $$

10. $$ 5x^2 + 13x + 6 $$
11. $$ 5x^2 + 13x - 6 $$
12. $$ 5x^2 - 13x + 6 $$

13. $$ x^2 + 10x + 24 $$
14. $$ x^2 - 10x + 24 $$
15. $$ x^2 - 10x - 24 $$

16. $$ 2x^2 + 10x + 12 $$
17. $$ 2x^2 - 12 + 10x  $$
18. $$ 10x + 2x^2 - 12 $$

19. $$ 3x^2 + 10x + 8 $$
20. $$ - 8 + 3x^2 - 10x  $$
21. $$ 10x - 8 + 3x^2  $$
