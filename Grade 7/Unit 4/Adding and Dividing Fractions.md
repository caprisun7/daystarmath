# Adding and Dividing Fractions

## Example 1

**Problem**

Simplify

$$
\frac{2x^2 + x - 1}{-4x^2 - 3x + 1} \times \frac{1 - 16x^2}{2x^2 - x}
$$

**Solution**

$$
\begin{align*}
 &= \frac{(2x - 1)(x + 1)}{(1 - 4x)(x + 1)} \times \frac{(1 + 4x)(1 - 4x)}{x(2x - 1)} \\
&= \frac{1 + 4x}{x}
\end{align*}
$$

## Practice 1.1

**Example**

Simplify

$$
\frac{1}{2t - 4} \times \frac{6 + t - 2t^2}{1 - t}
$$

**Answer**

$$
\frac{2t + 3}{2(t - 1)}
$$
